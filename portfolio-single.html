Description
The company ANIMUS, a world leader in multi-level telecommunications, is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing requirements of this market. Within this context, it regularly buys out other companies, notably SMEs. It recently learned that a company called ABSTERGO, specialized in next-generation Bluetooth protocols, was in difficulty. This represented a great opportunity for ANIMUS to further expand its activities, and it quickly acquired ABSTERGO. As per standard procedure with each buyout, it is planned to integrate ABSTERGO's information system into that of ANIMUS. The process is always the same: - the new company's system environment is linked to the ANIMUS environment; - the security settings are configured on the model defined by ANIMUS; - the final optimization checks are performed and the procedures are updated.
